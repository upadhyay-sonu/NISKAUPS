FILE 3: server/data/fallbackNews.json (NEW)
================================================================================

Created new file with 15 fallback articles:
- 5 articles in "topNews" category
- 5 articles in "bookNews" category  
- 4 articles in "trendingNews" category

Each article includes:
- title: Article headline
- description: Short summary
- image: Stock image URL (Unsplash)
- source: Article source name
- url: Link to article
- publishedAt: ISO timestamp
- provider: "Fallback" marker

PURPOSE: Ensure news always displays even if all APIs are down
